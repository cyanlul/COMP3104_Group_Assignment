DevOps Course Information:
The DevOps Course teaches us the importance of DevOps culture and it's processes. When these processes are applied correctly they can:
- Drastically reduce IT overhead
- Remove manual tasks both of a mundane or complex nature
- Enfore best security practices
- Expedite product delivery
- Improve communication and collaboration across teams

Essentially, this course is teaching how to make your life easier when working in the industry by using these processes.
