George Brown College is a post-secondary college that is located in Toronto and has three campuses.
One is located in Casa-Loma, Waterfront, and St. James. It is a college of Applied Arts
and Technology with three full campuses in downtown Toronto. George Brown College was charted in 1966
by the Government of Ontario and opened the next year in 1967. The college offers a variety of programs
and offers 35 diploma programs, 31 advanced diploma programs, as well as eight degree programs.
