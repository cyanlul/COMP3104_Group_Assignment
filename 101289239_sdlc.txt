The Importance of DevOps engineer in software development cycle:
DevOps Engineers hold a grand importance in software development cycles. These engineers improve processes and tasks
made for the SDLC while providing many benefits while that life cycle is being maintained. The culture of DevOps and
its processes allow for reduction of IT overhead, the removal of manual tasks that are both mudane or of a complex nature,
enforce securitiy practices that prevent breaches that could occur, expedite product delivery, and improve communication
and collaboration across teams. With these benefits being availiable when you have a DevOps engineer in the SDLC tasks
and processes are speed up and the organization of the project is at an extreme high. DevOps engineer's processes and their 
methodology also allows for a reduction of complexity, which as a result, allows for accelerated innovation between teams
during development and maintenance.
